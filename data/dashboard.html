✅ PRE-FLIGHT CHECK

💡 Last chance to change anything! Review your test plan below. Once you hit the big green button, the system takes over and guides you through every run. Read the directions — they tell you exactly what to do.
#CaseCarConditionTrialWeight (g)

📋 How To Run Each Test

  1. Check the screen — it tells you which car and weight to use
  2. Place the car at the top of the track behind the start sensor
  3. Make sure nothing is blocking the start or finish sensors
  4. Let go of the car when the screen says ARMED (don't push it!)
  5. Catch the car at the bottom — do NOT let it roll back through the sensor
  6. Wait — the system auto-loads the next run. You'll hear an announcement.
  7. If the system says something looks wrong, choose KEEP (save it) or RETRY (do it again)
